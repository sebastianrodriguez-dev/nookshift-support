Do not send passwords, Apple Account credentials, payment information, recovery codes, or verification codes.

NookShift Plus

NookShift Plus is a one-time purchase handled by Apple. Open Settings → NookShift Plus and use Restore Purchases if you already bought it with the same Apple Account.

Apple controls billing, refunds, and payment methods. NookShift does not receive your card details.

Reminders

NookShift uses local notifications. Permission is requested only after you choose to enable a reminder. Notification access can be changed in iOS Settings.

Calendar

On iOS 17 or later, NookShift can open Apple’s event editor for a planned room reset. NookShift does not read your calendars. Nothing is saved unless you confirm it in Apple’s editor.

Deleting app data

NookShift stores rooms, reset plans, reminders, purchase continuity, and activity history on your device. Deleting the app removes its local app data. Calendar events already saved through Apple’s editor must be removed in Apple Calendar.

Response time

This is an independent app. Support messages are reviewed as soon as practical, but immediate responses are not guaranteed.
